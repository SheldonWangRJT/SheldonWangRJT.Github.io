## âœ… STAR Structure

### Situation
Product goal was clear, but requirements changed weekly and multiple stakeholders disagreed.

### Task
Deliver usable v1 without waiting for full certainty.

### Action

- Created assumption log (explicit, reviewable, dated)
- Proposed phased scope (must-have vs nice-to-have)
- Added decision checkpoints every 1-2 weeks
- Built thin vertical slice to validate critical unknowns early

### Result

- Shipped v1 on time
- Fewer late rework cycles
- Stakeholders trusted process due to transparent assumption tracking
