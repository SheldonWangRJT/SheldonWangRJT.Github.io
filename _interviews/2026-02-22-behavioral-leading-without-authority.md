## âœ… STAR Answer Template

### Situation
Cross-team initiative was blocked because iOS, backend, and QA had conflicting priorities.

### Task
As senior IC, you needed alignment, delivery plan, and risk visibility without formal authority.

### Action

1. Mapped stakeholders and clarified incentives  
2. Proposed shared success metric and weekly decision cadence  
3. Broke work into owner-tagged milestones  
4. Escalated only unresolved tradeoffs, not every disagreement  

### Result

- Delivered milestone on schedule
- Reduced cross-team blocker age
- Established repeatable operating rhythm used by later projects
