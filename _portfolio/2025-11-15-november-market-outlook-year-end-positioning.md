## üí° Year-End Tax and Portfolio Strategies

### **Tax-Loss Harvesting**

November is an ideal time for tax-loss harvesting:

1. **Review Positions**: Identify positions with unrealized losses
2. **30-Day Rule**: Be mindful of wash sale rules
3. **Offset Gains**: Use losses to offset capital gains
4. **Carry Forward**: Unused losses can carry forward to future years

**Example Strategy**:
- Sell losing positions before year-end
- Wait 31 days before repurchasing (or buy similar but not identical securities)
- Use losses to offset gains from winning positions

### **Rebalancing Considerations**

Year-end is a natural time to rebalance portfolios:

| Portfolio Element | Action | Rationale |
|------------------|--------|-----------|
| **Asset Allocation** | Rebalance to target | Maintain risk profile |
| **Sector Weights** | Trim winners, add to laggards | Mean reversion potential |
| **Geographic Exposure** | Review international allocation | Diversification benefits |
| **Cash Position** | Optimize cash levels | Balance opportunity vs. safety |
